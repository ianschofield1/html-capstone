My Favorite Hobbies!

Soccer

Soccer is one of my favorite activities to do and it is a sport I grew up playing. The technique of the beautiful game always interested me; it is simple yet so intricate. I played all throughout my childhood and went on to play for my high school team in my teenage years. While I never made it to the collegiate/professional level, soccer will always be in my blood and in my heart; I encourage anyone that is interested to grab a ball and give it a try!

Chess

Much like soccer, this game involves a certain complexity aspect that creates an addicting environment for those who indulge. I enjoy playing others in my spare time, and learning more about new openings and middle game strategies. It is truly a beautiful game that keeps the mind extremely focused on the objective.

Listening to Podcasts

Pdocasts are enjoyable for me, as they provide an easily accessible way to learn new information. Whether I am driving from home to my job, or taking a long trip down to South Carolina, podcasts are a great way of finding relaxation whilst obtaining new information. There are also a wide variety of genres and subjects, so the options to listen to your desired subject are limitless!
